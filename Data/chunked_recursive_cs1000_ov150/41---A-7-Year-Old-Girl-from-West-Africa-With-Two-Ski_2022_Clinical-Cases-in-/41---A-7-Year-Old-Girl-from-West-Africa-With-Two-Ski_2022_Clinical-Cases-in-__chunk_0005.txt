The indication for surgical debridement depends on the clinical picture, but also on the availability of adequate anaesthetic and surgical care.

BU-specific antimycobacterial therapy should be commenced as soon as possible. In the absence of systemic symptoms, the toxicity of the recommended antibiotic treatment may justify a few days of delay until diagnostic results have been obtained.
