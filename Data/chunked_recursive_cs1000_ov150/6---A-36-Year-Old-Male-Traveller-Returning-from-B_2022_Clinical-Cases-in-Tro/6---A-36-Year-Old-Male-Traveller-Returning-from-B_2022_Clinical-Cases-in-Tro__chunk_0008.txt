Gnathostomiasis is confirmed when demonstration of the parasite is made microscopically, radiologically or on positive serological examination up to 3 months after presentation. Lumbar puncture and cranial imaging may be necessary in suspected CNS disease. CSF is often xanthochromic and may show eosinophilia.
