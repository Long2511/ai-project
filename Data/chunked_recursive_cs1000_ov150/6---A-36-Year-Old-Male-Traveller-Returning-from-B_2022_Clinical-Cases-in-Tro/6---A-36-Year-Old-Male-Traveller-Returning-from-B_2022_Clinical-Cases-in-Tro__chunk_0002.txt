The patient's total white cell count was 8.9 ×10⁹/L, within the reference range of 4–10. The eosinophil count was elevated at 0.9 × 10⁹/L, compared to a normal value of less than 0.5. Liver function tests, creatinine, and electrolytes were normal, and HIV antibodies were negative.

A 36-year-old European presents because of a mobile migratory mass he has been noticing for the past 3 weeks. Five weeks ago, he returned from a fishing trip to the Okavango Delta. Two of his travel companions have the same symptoms. Full blood count shows eosinophilia.
