The most important differential diagnoses in the given set-ting are MDR TB, histoplasmosis and paracoccidioidomyco-sis. TB is highly endemic in Peru and MDR TB is an increasing issue. TB and MDR TB almost always need to be considered when clinical symptoms and evolution, as in this case, are suggestive of the disease. What would be unusual about this patient is his origin from a rural area in the central Peruvian Amazon where MDR TB is still uncom-mon in comparison with underprivileged districts of Lima and other big cities of Peru.
