A 46-year-old woman, 158cm, 65kg (BMI 26kg/m²), afebrile, blood pressure 130/80 mmHg, pulse 80bpm.
Enlarged, tender cervical, nuchal, inguinal and axillary lymph nodes. Massive, tender swelling of the upper eyelids. Disseminated fluctuant cutaneous and subcutaneous abscesses with surrounding erythema, discharge of pus on slight pressure. Painful swelling of the left elbow. Otherwise normal physical examination.

On admission, laboratory results showed a white cell count of 30.8 x 10⁹/L, which was above the reference range of 3.8-11. The haemoglobin level was 8.2 g/dL, below the reference of 12.3-15.3. The mean corpuscular volume was 88 fL, within the normal range of 80-94. The platelet count was elevated at 592 x 10⁹/L, compared to a reference range of 150-400. The C-reactive protein was 267 mg/L, significantly higher than the reference of less than 5, and the erythrocyte sedimentation rate was over 110 mm/h, far exceeding the reference value of less than 20.
