The patient was diagnosed with paucibacillary leprosy and commenced on rifampicin and dapsone which he took for 6 months. The sensory neuropathy was treated with oral prednisolone 30 mg daily which was reduced by 5 mg each month. The patient was counselled extensively about the cause of leprosy and that he was not an infection risk to his family or friends, who were examined and had no signs of leprosy. He was advised how to care for his hands and avoid trauma. He made a full recovery.
