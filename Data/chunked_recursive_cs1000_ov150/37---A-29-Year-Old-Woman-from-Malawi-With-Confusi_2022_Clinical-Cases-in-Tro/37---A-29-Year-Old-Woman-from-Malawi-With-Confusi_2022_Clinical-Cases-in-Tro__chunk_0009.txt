Apart from a poor diet, pellagra may be caused by malabsorption, alcoholism, antituberculous treatment and other aetiologies. The roll-out of INH-preventive therapy (IPT) for HIV patients has led to an increase in pellagra cases in some areas in Africa. Single cases of pellagra continue to be described from all over the world.
