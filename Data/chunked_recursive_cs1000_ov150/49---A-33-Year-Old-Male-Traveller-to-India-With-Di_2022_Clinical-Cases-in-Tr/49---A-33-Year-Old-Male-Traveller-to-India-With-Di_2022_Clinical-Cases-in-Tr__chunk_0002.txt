Stool for reducing substances: positive. Stool examination for enteric parasites: Giardia trophozoites are seen in the fresh specimen. Giardia cysts are detected on formol-ether con-centrated specimens.

A 33-year-old Finnish traveller to India presents with passage of loose stools, four or five episodes a day for the past 2 weeks. The stools have become greasy and foul-smelling, and he has bloating and distension after consumption of milk or milk products.

The presence of Giardia trophozoites and cysts in the stool sample is confirmatory of giardiasis. The presence of reducing substances in the stool indicates a carbohydrate malabsorption, most likely of post-infectious origin.
