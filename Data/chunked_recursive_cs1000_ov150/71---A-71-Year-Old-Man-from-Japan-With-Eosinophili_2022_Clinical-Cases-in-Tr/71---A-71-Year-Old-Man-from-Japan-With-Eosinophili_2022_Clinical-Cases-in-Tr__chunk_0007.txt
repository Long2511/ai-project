Predominant Paragonimus species vary by region. In East Asia, the species are P. westermani and P. skrijabinia, also known as P. myazakii. In South and South-east Asia, the predominant species is P. heterotremus, though P. westermani is found in the Philippines. In Africa, the species are P. africanus and P. uterobilateralis. North America's species is P. kelicottii, and in Central and South America, it is P. mexicanus.
