A 27-year-old male Swiss tourist presents with persisting and incapacitating symmetrical polyarthralgias primarily affecting the small peripheral joints after returning from a trip to the Amazon Basin of northern Peru. The patient reports an acute self-limiting febrile illness accompanied by a rash before the onset of the polyarthralgias, which had clinically been diagnosed as dengue infection at a local hospital in Peru.
