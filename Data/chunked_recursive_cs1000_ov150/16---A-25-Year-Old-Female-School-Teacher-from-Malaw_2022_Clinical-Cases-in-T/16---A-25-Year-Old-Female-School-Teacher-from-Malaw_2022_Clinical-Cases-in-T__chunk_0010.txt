In the emergency department the patient received ceftriaxone 2g IV, 10 mg of diazepam IV and 600 mg of phenobarbitone IV. Her oxygen saturation improved with resuscitation. She received 10 days of IV ceftriaxone and recovered consciousness by day 3. CSF culture grew S. pneumoniae. Audiometry revealed a minor hearing loss in her right ear. No further neurological impairment or seizures were noted; anticonvulsants were weaned by day 3.
