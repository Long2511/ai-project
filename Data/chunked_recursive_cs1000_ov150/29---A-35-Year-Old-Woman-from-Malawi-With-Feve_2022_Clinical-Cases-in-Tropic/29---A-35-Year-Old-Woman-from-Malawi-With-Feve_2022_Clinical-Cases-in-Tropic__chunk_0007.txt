The baseline CD4 count should be checked and the patient should be started on co-trimoxazole preventive treatment as prophylaxis against Pneumocystis pneumonia, toxoplasmosis and other opportunistic infections. Antiretroviral therapy (ART) should be started as soon as possible, once the acute infection is under control and concomitant opportunistic infections have been ruled out. The latter is important to prevent unmasking immune reconstitution inflammatory syndrome (IRIS). Her children and her current and past sexual partners should be encouraged to go for an HIV test.
